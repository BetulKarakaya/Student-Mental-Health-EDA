# 🖼️ Visual Insights Repository

This directory contains the key visual findings extracted from the student mental health and dropout risk analysis. These visualizations are designed to highlight both statistical distributions and the complex interactions between variables.

### 📊 Highlighted Figures

1. **Correlation Matrix:**
   - Illustrates the strong linear relationship between psychological factors (`burnout`, `depression`) and `dropout_risk`, while highlighting the negligible impact of demographic variables.

2. **Risk Drivers Analysis:**
   - Visualizes the density of high-risk students under financial stress and academic pressure using comparative distribution plots.

3. **Mental Health vs. Academic Success:**
   - Scatter and box plots demonstrating that academic success alone is not a protective buffer against dropout risk when burnout levels are high.

### 🛠️ Technical Details
- **Libraries Used:** Plotly (Interactive) and Seaborn (Statistical).
- **Format:** High-resolution PNG.
- **Utility:** These visuals serve as empirical evidence for strategic reports and presentations aimed at educational stakeholders.
